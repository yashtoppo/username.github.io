A Letter From My Heart

To my dearest Your Love,

On this beautiful day that the world gifted you to it — and to me — I find myself overwhelmed with a love so full it spills over every quiet moment, every ordinary morning, every glance across the room.

You are everything I never thought to ask for and everything I never want to stop finding. Your laugh is my favourite sound. Your presence is my favourite place. And your love — your love is the softest, bravest thing I have ever known.

I am so endlessly proud of you. Of the strength you carry so quietly, the light you give so freely, the way you love with your whole heart without even realising how rare that is.

Today and every day, I choose you. I choose us. I choose every version of you — the radiant one, the tired one, the dreaming one, the one who needs to be held.

Happy Birthday, my love. May this year give back even a fraction of the joy you bring to everyone around you — especially to me.

Forever yours, Your Baccha ❤️
